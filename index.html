About

Perth-based Designer specialising in User Interface & Experience Design and UX Research. I love using Design Thinking and Human Centred Design approaches to solve business problems and help build the right products. My design style is very minimalist and I enjoy turning complex processes into simple, usable solutions. Industries I have experience workings with include mining, health and financial services. When I'm not doing Design work I'm usually reading about Design, watching architecture shows or trying (and failing) to learn another language.
